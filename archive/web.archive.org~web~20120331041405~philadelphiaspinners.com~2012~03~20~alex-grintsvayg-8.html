Alex Grintsvayg – #8

Defense - Cutter

Alex Grintsvayg

What is your home town?
Kiev, Ukraine

How many years have you played Ultimate?
6

Where did you go to school?
Stuyvesant High School
Rennselaer Polytechnic Institute

How did you start playing?
My college had a Freshman Olympics competition for all incoming freshmen, and one of the events was ultimate. I signed up on a whim and our team ended up winning the tournament. The guys from the college team were there to supervise and help us out. They convinced me to try out for the team, and in a few weeks I was completely hooked on ultimate.

What is your dream when it comes to Ultimate?
To see an ultimate player on a Wheaties box.

What athlete do you consider a great role model?
My grandfather

What is your favorite play in Ultimate (huck, greatest. layout D, etc) and why?
The Rochester. It’s surprisingly effective and no one sees it coming.

Anything you want your fans to know?
In Natoma, Kansas, it’s illegal to throw knives at men wearing striped suits.
